Users

We all need a little help. And if you think you don't need help now, at some point you'll need employees, consultants, designers, or an assistant. With Breeasy for Business - we know you can't do it alone and we make it easy to add users to your account for Free - No per user or seat fees to complicate your life. Once added, you can assign tasks to your your and they can assign them to you and each other. Collaboration is easy and baked right into Breeasy for Business.

Breeasy Users can:

Collaborate on Projects and CSIs

Comunicate Effectively

Track To Dos, Tasks, and Events

Log Calls, Create Notes, Add Contacts and Companies

Increase Productivity

Users are at the heart of Breeasy for Business and you get up to 5 users with our Standard Plan. Your users are interchangeable. You can remove and add as much as you like. If you need more than 5, upgrade to our Deluxe plan and the number of users is unlimited. To give you a sense how powerful and easy managing users can be, you can add up to five users with our Risk-Free Trial.
