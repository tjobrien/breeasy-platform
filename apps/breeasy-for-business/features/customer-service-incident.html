Customer Service Incident

Customer service is the heart of any business. It costs too much to acquire customers and then lose them because of poor customer service. Customer expect quick acknowledgement of their issue and real, substantial effort when dealing with it. Breeasy for Business has a unique tool for dealing with these issues - Customer Service Incidents - or CSI.

With CSIs you can:

Assign to an employee for resolution

View CSI status right from the Dashboard

Track multiple resolutions per issue

Measure resolution rates among your users

Create a custom Resolution Knowledge Base

CSIs are one of the most useful and measurable features with Breeasy for Business. Available with Standard and Deluxe Plans, managing your small business customer issues and problems is Easy with Breeasy! And with some Risk-Free Trial love.
