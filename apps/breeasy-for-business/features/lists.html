Lists

We love lists. Lists for shopping, for regular tasks, or a list to make sure we organize our thoughts in a logical way. Lists at Breeasy are one of the most flexible and useful features of the popular Breeasy for Business app.

You can create lists, share them with your colleagues, attach them to a task, email them, copy them. How you use them is up to you. But be warned, lists are addicting, and once you start, you'll be creating lists for everything - and be more efficient.

With Lists you can:

Attach it to a Task

Share it with other users in your company

Don't repeat yourself - Create new lists from current ones

Mark items complete

Email it!

Lists and more lists. There is no limit to the number of lists you can have or create with Breeasy for Business. Already a Business for Breeasy user? Please visit our forums and let us know what you think.
