Locations

Sometimes we all need a change of scenery - get out of the office, mix it up a bit. But we are creatures of habit - once we find a place we like, we tend to go back. That's where Breeasy locations can help. You can enter your favorite places to meet, have lunch, or just relax. Next time you schedule an event, that location is there for you, and it even includes an handy map.

Locations are shared among all the users in your account. This makes it easy for everyone to meet at the same place. To make sure they know the time, you can easily create an event.

Locations is one the many features available with Breeasy for Business Standard and Deluxe Plans. You can enter as many locations as you like, and can link them to events - Now, no one will ever got lost when you schedule a meeting!.
